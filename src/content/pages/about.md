Jailbreaks FYI is a practitioner reference for LLM jailbreak techniques — working bypasses sourced and dated, the model behaviors they exploit, the patches that followed, and the detection strategies that defenders have tried.

## What we cover

- **Working bypass techniques** — documented with the specific models affected and conditions required
- **Model behavior analysis** — the underlying behaviors that create exploitable conditions
- **Patch tracking** — what vendors fixed, when, and whether the fix held
- **Detection strategies** — approaches defenders have tried and their measured effectiveness
- **Disclosure timelines** — who found it, when, and how it was disclosed

## Who reads this

Researchers, red teamers, and model developers who want a clear, sourced view of the jailbreak landscape as it actually exists — with honest assessments of what still works and what has been patched.

## Stay current

Subscribe to the RSS feed for new technique entries. Tips on newly discovered or disclosed techniques are welcome — contact the editorial desk with reproducible details and a disclosure timeline.
